Mary Calhoun Family

Mary Calhoun Clayton (my grandmother) and her parents and brothers and sisters and her grandmother. Front row, L-R; Jim (father), Irene (mother), Bettie Clements (Mary Calhoun’s grandmother and thus my great-great grandmother). Back row, L-R; Everett, Arthur, Bessie, Muriel, Estelle, Bernard, Eula, Mary, Lillian, Bob, Bill. Not sure when this was taken but I’d say in the 1930s. Great-great-grandmother Bettie Clements died in 1942 so the picture was before that date.
All the Clayton children with their baptismal gown. Every one of them used the same gown which is now in the possession of Joan. This picture was taken around May of 2001 at Janice’s house. Front row, L-R; Jean, Janice, Judy Middle row, L-R; Joan, Joyce, Bobby Back row, L-R; Jimmy, Johnny, Joe-Bill While grandmother took very good care of this gown, the girls would still sneak it out of storage and dress up their dolls in it. I’m sure capital punishment would follow afterwards.
